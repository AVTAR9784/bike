About Us

Trusted & Faster Logistic Service Provider

GALAXY WORLDWIDE CONTAINER LINE is the leading international freight forwarding company that ensures reliable and safe delivery of goods in various international markets. Since its inception in 2017, the company has grown substantially and now, has a strong grip over the major international markets including China, South East Asia, Middle East, Europe, and the USA. Our wide and comprehensive logistic network offers a single step solution to your air, water and road freight forwarding needs. Our complete spectrum of distribution services include Sea Freight, Air freight, Storage & Distribution, Customs clearance, Door-to-door Logistics, Ex-Im consultancy, World Wide Groupage, Outbound Groupage, Inbound Groupage, DDU & DDP Services, Break Bulk & Project Cargo
